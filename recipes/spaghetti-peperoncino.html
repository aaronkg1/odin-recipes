Spaghetti peperoncino

Description

One of the simplest yet most delicious pasta dishes you can make. The epitome of italian cooking!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic
  • Red Chilli (or chilli flakes)
  • Olive Oil
  • Parsley
  • Lemon
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Bring large pan of water to the boil and season generously with salt. Add spaghetti.
  2. Meanwhile, finely slice garlic and chilli and saute on low heat with olive oil. To avoid burning garlic, add spoonfuls of pasta water as necessary. Cook until garlic has softened and lost its raw edge.
  3. Reserve a mugful of pasta water and once pasta is aldente, add pasta to garlic and chilli.
  4. Gradually add pasta water while mixing to form a silky emulsion.
  5. Mix in the zest of the lemon and finally chopped parsley, along with lemon juice to taste.
  6. Serve with grated parmesan.
