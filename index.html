Lasagne

Description

This easy lasagne recipe is great for when you don't have much time. Whack it in the oven and relax with a nice glass of wine!

Ingredients

  • Brown Onion
  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Minced Beef (or vegetarian alternative)
  • Garlic
  • Pasatta
  • Tomato Puree
  • Oregano
  • Red Wine (Optional)
